Bleeding Control

Symptoms:

pain at the injured site.
swollen, tight abdomen.
nausea and vomiting.
pale, clammy, sweaty skin.
breathlessness.

Remedies:

Mix 2 teaspoons of raw, unfiltered apple cider vinegar in a glass of water. Drink this mixture twice or thrice a day during the periods for the best results. Add a teaspoon of dried red raspberry leaves to a cup of boiling water. Strain the leaves and drink the tea twice daily. You can even add a few drops of honey to the mixture if needed. You can consume ginger in either raw form or the form of tea. Add spinach, fish, and pumpkin seeds to your diet.

Ingredients:

apple cider vinegar
dried red raspberry leaves
honey
ginger
spinach
fish
pumpkin seeds
