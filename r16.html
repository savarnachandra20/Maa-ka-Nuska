Cramps

Symptoms:

leg swelling
redness
Pain that radiates to your lower back and thighs

Remedies:

Muscular cramps: 1. Apply clove oil on the affected parts.
2. Apply clove oil as poultice on the affected areas
Period cramps:
1. A piece of fresh ginger (adrak), ground and boiled in a cup of water. The infusion is taken thrice daily after meals along with sugar
2. Cut a banana into half, put some in between cardamom along with sugar or honey and eat it like a sandwich.

Ingredients:

clove oil
ginger
banana
cardamom
sugar
honey
