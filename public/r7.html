Strep Throat

Symptoms:

Throat pain that usually comes on quickly.
Painful swallowing.
Red and swollen tonsils, sometimes with white patches or streaks of pus.
Tiny red spots on the area at the back of the roof of the mouth

Remedies:

1. Apply liquorice (mulathi) paste like a paint around the throat for relief.
2. Eat a plain betel leaf (pan) with liquorice (mulathi) 2-3 times a day.
3. Drink tea boiled with ginger (adrak) and a few tulsi leaves 2-3 times a day.
4. Gargle with warm salt water at least twice a day. However do not make gargling sounds as this may aggravate the soreness.
5. Pound 2-3 cloves (laung), garlic (lasan) and add to a cupful of honey. Keep for 1-2 days. Have one teaspoon thrice a day.
6. Drink lots of water (10-12 glasses) a day since most throat problems are intensified by dehydration.
7. Have the mixture of ½ teaspoon honey and ½ teaspoon lemon juice every 1-2 hours.
8. Add two tablespoon of fenugreek (methi) seeds to 6 cups of water. Heat on low flame/simmer for 15-20 minutes. Cool to bearable temperature. Strain and gargle 2-3 times a day with this.
9. Heat a cup of milk (warm). Add 1-2 pinch of turmeric powder (haldi) mix well and drink at might.

Ingredients:

liquorice
betel leaf
ginger
cloves
garlic
honey
lemon juice
fenugreek
milk
turmeric powder
milk
