>Meningitis

Symptoms:

Sudden high fever.
Stiff neck.
Severe headache that seems different from normal.
Headache with nausea or vomiting.
Confusion or difficulty concentrating.
Seizures.

Remedies:

• Take 3-4 cloves of garlic each day or you can extract the garlic juice and consume it with any fruit or vegetable juice.
• Consuming soybean milk can help improve your immune system and speed up the healing process of meningitis.

Ingredients:

Clove of garlic
any fruit
soybean milk
